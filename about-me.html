Jessie McCanlas

Greetings! I am a Communication Technology student at UTA with a passion for User Experience design. I am set to graduate in December 2023, and my academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in graphic design, web design, and User Experience design.

My passion revolves around crafting digital products that seamlessly blend user-friendliness with aesthetic appeal. My ultimate aspiration is to establish myself as a User Experience designer, leveraging my skills to enhance the accessibility, intuitiveness, and overall enjoyment of websites and applications for all users. I firmly believe that exceptional design possesses the transformative potential to impact the world positively, and I am thrilled to be a part of this catalytic force for change.
